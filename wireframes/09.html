Popular Questions

Planning & Agenda

How do I add a new driving lesson?

Click on the desired time slot in the agenda. Choose the student and instructor. Save the appointment to confirm.

Planning & Agenda

Can I automatically repeat lessons?

Yes, when creating a lesson you can choose the 'Repeat' option. Here you can set the frequency and end date.

Instructors

How do I link an instructor to a vehicle?

Go to Settings > Instructors. Select the instructor and choose the default vehicle from the dropdown menu.

Billing

How do I send an invoice to a student?

Go to the student profile, Invoices tab. Click 'New Invoice' and select the outstanding lessons or packages.

Quotes

How do I create a new quote?

Click the 'New Quote' button in the dashboard. Fill in the customer details and product lines and click send.

Customer Management

Can I import customers from Excel?

Yes, go to Customers > Import. Upload your .csv or .xlsx file and map the columns to the correct fields.
